# Build a Cinematic Scroll-Controlled 3D Landing Page

## Goal

Create a premium, cinematic, highly-performant landing page using:

* Next.js 15+
* React Three Fiber (R3F)
* Three.js
* GSAP + ScrollTrigger
* Framer Motion (optional for UI)
* Tailwind CSS

The page should feel like a luxury digital art experience rather than a normal website.

---

# Visual Theme

Background:

* Pure black void
* Deep-space atmosphere
* Sparse galaxy particles
* Subtle volumetric fog
* Tiny distant stars
* No visible horizon
* High contrast cinematic lighting

Color Palette:

* Black: #000000
* Neon Blue: #00D4FF
* Electric Cyan: #39E7FF
* Neon White: #FFFFFF

---

# Scene Structure

The entire experience happens in one continuous scene.

Sections are revealed through scrolling.

Use ScrollTrigger to drive all animations.

---

# Stage 1: Initial Load

Camera:

* Fixed frontal view
* Slight cinematic perspective
* Smooth floating motion

Text:

LOREM IPSUM

Large center-aligned heading.

Typography:

* Extruded 3D text
* Premium modern font
* Thick geometry
* Sharp edges

Initial State:

Every letter starts above the viewport.

Example:

L
O
R
E
M

All positioned vertically off-screen.

---

# Stage 2: Letter Drop Animation

On page load:

Letters fall individually from the top.

Requirements:

* Sequential timing
* Slight delay between letters
* Physics-inspired easing
* Small bounce on landing
* Tiny camera shake on impact

Order:

L
O
R
E
M

then

I
P
S
U
M

Each letter settles into the final heading position.

---

# Stage 3: Scroll Begins

As user scrolls:

A realistic spray paint can enters from top center.

Requirements:

* Fully modeled 3D spray can
* Metallic body
* Slight reflections
* Rotates naturally

The can follows a predefined spline path.

---

# Stage 4: Neon Paint Reveal

While scrolling:

The spray can paints the text.

Implementation:

Create a shader-based reveal mask.

Before painting:

* Text appears dark metallic
* Almost invisible

As spray passes:

* Neon blue paint appears
* Paint reveal follows spray position
* Surface gradually fills

Not a simple opacity animation.

Must feel like actual paint deposition.

---

# Stage 5: Edge Illumination

Immediately after paint reaches an area:

Neon white light travels along text edges.

Requirements:

* Animated emissive outline
* Border glow propagation
* Moving light streak

Effect:

Like electricity flowing around the letter contours.

Use custom shader or postprocessing.

---

# Stage 6: Particle Spray System

Spray can emits:

* Paint particles
* Mist particles
* Tiny sparks

Particle behavior:

* Velocity-based
* GPU particles preferred
* Fade naturally
* Motion blur effect

Performance target:

60 FPS.

---

# Stage 7: Galaxy Interaction

While text becomes painted:

Nearby galaxy particles react.

Behavior:

* Subtle attraction
* Blue illumination
* Energy ripple effect

The text should feel like an energy source activating.

---

# Stage 8: Full Text Activation

At ~70% scroll:

Text fully painted.

State:

* Bright neon blue surface
* White glowing edges
* Bloom effect
* Reflections
* Floating energy particles

This should be the visual climax.

---

# Stage 9: Spray Can Descent

Continue scrolling.

Spray can:

* Finishes painting
* Slowly descends vertically
* Moves to exact screen center
* Rotates to face viewer

Background darkens further.

Particles gather around can.

Build anticipation.

---

# Stage 10: Energy Charge-Up

Before explosion:

Can begins charging.

Effects:

* Pulsing blue light
* Increasing glow intensity
* Orbiting particles
* Light distortion
* Camera vibration

Duration:

~2 seconds scroll-controlled.

---

# Stage 11: Neon Firework Explosion

At final scroll point:

Can explodes into a massive energy burst.

NOT a realistic fire explosion.

Instead:

* Neon blue explosion
* Cyan plasma rings
* White energy streaks
* Galaxy shockwave
* Volumetric particle bloom

Visual references:

* Tron
* Arcane magical effects
* Sci-fi energy detonation

Requirements:

* Multiple particle layers
* Expanding rings
* Bloom
* Light flash

---

# Stage 12: Fade Out

After explosion:

* Particles dissolve
* Glow fades
* Text slowly disappears
* Galaxy returns to darkness

Final frame:

Pure black void.

---

# Technical Requirements

Use:

* React Three Fiber
* Drei
* GSAP ScrollTrigger
* Postprocessing

Postprocessing:

* Bloom
* Chromatic Aberration (subtle)
* Depth of Field
* Vignette

Avoid:

* Heavy CPU particle systems
* DOM-based animations
* Excessive React rerenders

Use:

* InstancedMesh
* GPU particles
* Memoization
* Shader materials

---

# Scroll Architecture

0–20% Scroll:
Letter drops

20–60% Scroll:
Paint reveal

60–80% Scroll:
Edge glow activation

80–90% Scroll:
Can descent

90–100% Scroll:
Charge-up and explosion
